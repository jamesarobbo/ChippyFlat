#Be Brave

Chippy Worldwide Racing is a community of like-minded people that love travel, love road-trips and love venturing off the beaten track to explore unusual destinations. Through social media Chippy Worldwide Racing provides a platform for our members to team up, share experiences and provide one another with trip advice.

Founded by petrol-head, Lizzy Mae and navigator extraordinaire, Valentina, Chippy WorldWide Racing began life as a way for the two friends to have fun together while seeing the world. Taking road rally racing to the extreme the pair have so far driven through 39 countries, often with few breaks but always making sure to immerse themselves in the local culture whenever and wherever they do stop.

Endurance

Anything is possible when you put your mind to it. Lizzy Mae drove 26 hours without a break from Albania to Switzerland - the definition of extreme road rally racing.

Pioneer

A pioneering spirit in modern society is rare. Back in 2013, Chippy became the first MINI Cooper to drive through Transnistria, a war-torn region of Moldova.

Limitless

Only you decide your limits. The team are always pushing their boundaries; on a recent trip through Germany, Lizzy Mae reached 220km/h on the Autobahn in the team's MINI Cooper!

Pilot

Lizzy is the pilot and the passion behind the wheel. From a young age growing up in Switzerland her love for cars was clear and at 18 her dream car, "Chippy", arrived not a day too late. Armed with just the strength of her resolve and a hunger for driving, nothing stands in the way of Lizzy and her destination. From Odessa to Vienna and Lisbon to Milan, Lizzy will not only lead the way but make any destination feel like home.

Vehicle

At the heart of Chippy WorldWide Racing is "Chippy"; a 2009 Mini Cooper S Cabriolet. He may be small but he is mighty, always safely guiding his passengers through the harshest of environments. Co-pilot

Valentina, co-pilot, financier, stylist and Mother Russia native, is the energy of the team. Always equipped with an emergency snack, energy drink or anti-sleeping chewing gum she keeps the team focused and firing on all cylinders. And with her style, the team blends seamlessly into local cultures, from Gypsy hipster in Romania to gangster chic in Moldova.
